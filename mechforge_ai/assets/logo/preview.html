Design Concept

⚙️ Gear

The outer gear represents mechanical engineering and precision manufacturing - the core focus of MechForge.

🧠 AI Core

The central circuit pattern symbolizes artificial intelligence and neural networks powering the platform.

🎨 Gradient

The blue-to-purple gradient represents innovation, technology, and the fusion of traditional engineering with modern AI.

✨ Nodes

The glowing nodes represent data points, knowledge connections, and the interconnected nature of engineering systems.
